# Roadmap

## Phase 1

- authentication and identity flows
- organizations, teams, and tenant isolation
- boards, columns, cards, and activity history
- manager assignment workflow

## Phase 2

- collaborators, comments, subtasks, and attachments
- projects and workstreams
- aggregated manager board views

## Phase 3

- reporting cycles
- activity entries
- personal MSR generation, editing, and submission

## Phase 4

- team MSR compilation and approval
- dashboards and analytics
- notifications and reminders
- PDF, Word, CSV, and print exports

## Phase 5

- admin controls and platform support tools
- templates and branding controls
- audit review and policy hardening
- test coverage and release automation

## Future Enhancements

- Slack and Microsoft Teams integrations
- richer search indexing
- advanced analytics and forecasting
- AI-assisted summaries behind a safe interface
- SSO and enterprise identity providers
- retention policies and legal hold workflows
